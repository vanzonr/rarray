ECNST should not depend on a TLIKE. This may or may not be causing the
streambug.  But in any case, ECNST shold not depend on TLIKE, because
TLIKE allows everything, not just stuff that can be converted into T.

To get T-like behavior, one must define the T-like functions as
inline friends that take a T. Such friends functions are free (i.e.,
non-template), and are generate at class instantiation. Because they
are free they will take T-like arguments as well, as long as one of
the arguments is a complete match.
